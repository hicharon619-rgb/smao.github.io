# SPACE IS THE PLACE — Reissued for 2026

## 1. Project Description
A digital editorial publication — a speculative planetary reissue of the 1974 film "Space Is the Place." This is a single-page scrolling website designed as a critical design fiction piece. It is not a film introduction or research report, but rather a "signal" transmitted from 1974 to 2026. The site combines poster-like design, editorial publication aesthetics, archival texture, and image-text confrontation to deliver a re-activated future narrative.

Target audience: Readers interested in afrofuturism, critical design, planetary thinking, and editorial publications.
